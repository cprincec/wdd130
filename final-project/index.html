Director's Message

You are welcome to NATIONAL SECONDARY SCHOOL AWKA. The home of academic Excellence.
National Secondary School is one of the oldest schools in Nigeria, located at Awka, capital city of Anambra State. The school was created to provide world-class education and utilizes modern technologies since its advent in catering for children and fulfilling its mission to:

  • To help students acquire knowledge, skills and ability to contribute to the development of the society.
  • To inculcate a positive moral life and spiritual value, to enable the students to develop interpersonal and human relationship.
  • To integrate the students with their culture, arts, languages and values.
  • To develop the students understanding, to appreciate and respect the opinions and feelings of others.
  • To promote higher level of discipline and integrity among the students.
  • To train students towards understanding and appreciating the dignity of labour.
  • To train students to live an exemplary life and to be good citizens of the country.
  • To prepare the children for future life by equipping them with the spirit of loyalty, dedication, intelligent and to be conscious of their responsibilities.
  • To produce people who can apply immediate knowledge to improve and find solutions to environmental and social problems.
