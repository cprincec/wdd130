. The only requirement is that you are not afraid of water. Usually you don’t swim in the river. In case of fall you do float along the river and the life vest keeps you on the surface. Your guide is fully trained, knows the safety instructions, and the river.

All participants under 18 years have to be in the boat with parent or guardian. During the high season – April, May, the kids could join depending on the moment conditions, like water level. In the summer months younger kids could join their families in the boat.

All guides are Certified according to the standards of the International Rafting Federation as whitewater guides for different river classes.

In order to secure your seat in a raft you just need to call us or send us an e-mail. After confirmation we will ask you for deposit. We will need your full names and your identity number for making insurance.
