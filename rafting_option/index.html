We run rivers to relax

Water is considered the elixir and source of life. It covers more than 70% of the Earth’s surface, makes up nearly 70% of our bodies, and constitutes over 70% of our heart and brains,” says Nichols. This has been shown to trigger an immediate response in our brains when we’re near water. In fact, the mere sight and sound of water can induce a flood of neurochemicals that promote wellness, increase blood flow to the brain and heart and induce relaxation.

We run white water for the rush

Break out the paddles and make sure you have a good grip. While whitewater rafting is a terrific recreation for the whole family, some rivers give more "rush and thrill" than "relax and chill." From the beautiful Colardo River to the thunderous Gauley River, we rounded up exciting rivers that are best known for offering heart-pounding adventures.

Come run with us!

Relax to the calming sound of gentle forest stream, birds singing in the background and the noise of flowing water over river rocks creating small waterfalls. Atmospheric peace and tranquility which can help relax the mind and body, enabling greater clarity and a more balanced and productive state. Come and feel the rush!!!
